It is mandatory that projects undertake periodic reviews of this questionnaire in order to avoid any possible risks that may eventuate due to changes in scope and/or solution. If during any future review, the answer to any question changes to YES, InfoSec must be engaged.

Networks have mandated that the Project Manager fill the Annexure I (owned and managed by Networks) as an evidence of periodic review of Networks initiated project.

However, even if all responses are NO but the Project Team believes that potential risk can be introduced to the Network, InfoSec can be engaged by providing relevant details in the section below. This will trigger full InfoSec risk assessment for the project.
